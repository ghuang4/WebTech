Gantry State Plaza Park

Experience one of Long Island City's Waterfront Parks! This area is becoming the new Manhattan with its sky-rise buildings and great places to eat and drink with friends. Gantry State Plaza Park is a 12 acre riverside oasis that boasts spectacular views of the midtown Manhattan Skyline which includes the Empire State Building and the United Nations. Visitors coming here can enjoy a relaxing stroll along the park's four piers, manicured gardens and unique mist fountain. In addition to these sights, visitors can also enjoy the rugged beauty of the park's centerpieces. The industrial monuments were actually once used to load and unload rail car floats and barges, reminding everyone of the unique past making the this park what it is today. If you walk along the waterfront, you can also get an amazing view of the city skyline and the gantries as a stage. There are even summer concerts and events that occur here, bringing in moments of joy and memories you will never forget. There are also recreational faciltiies such as basketball courts, playgrounds, handball courts and a fishing pier with its own cleaning table. Dogs are also welcome at the park, on leash or off, in the park's two dog runs.

Fun Activities to Enjoy!

Beach Volleyball

Near the left end of the park, there is a playground filled with a turf-field and beach volleyball court where you can find families playing, laughing and finding enthusiastic ways to get in their work outs. Enjoy a fun game of beach volleyball on these courts that visitors can use for free!

Fishing

Whether you're an experienced angler or a first time fisher, come try out the waters at Gantry State Plaza Park! Test out your basics skillsets on catching and releasing, reeling fish and collect a bundle to bring home. This activity is for all ages so you can enjoy time with family and friends!

Ride the NYC Ferry

Right next to the volleyball courts, you can take a ride on the NYC Ferry from Gantry State Plaza Park to Wall Street. The ride is approximately 34 minutes to get to these locations and it only costs $3. So enjoy all the views that New York City has to over along the waterfront through this ferry!

Basketball

This is another fun activity that you can enjoy with friends and family. You can enjoy a friendly game of basketball by the courts near the oval shaped turf field and play till your heart desires. Just bring your own ball or simply jump in a game with some new friends you can meet along the way!
